My X-mas Letter

Dear Santa,

Christmas is drawing near. Its a time of joy, happiness, is here. Houses are decorated, songs are sung, and cups are filled with cheer. All in anticipation of the arrival of jolly, magical essence cloaked in a red suit, with a sleigh pulled by reindeer.

So check your list, maybe check it twice. It will confirm I been just the right amount of naughty and mostly nice. So review the list of my christmas wishes.
